Project : Mindmate AI
Description: AI system for detecting cognitive rumination analysis with alexithymia. 
Features: 
- Notepad
- chat assistant
- Mind and emotion anlaysis
- Health
- Song suggester

Problem : People struggle to understand their emotions and often fall into overthinking loops. 

solution: MindMate AI is an AI-powered emotional intelligence support system designed to help users better understand their thoughts, emotional patterns, and cognitive behaviors in a non-clinical and safe environment.
The system focuses on analyzing user-written text (through chat or journaling) to detect patterns such as cognitive rumination (overthinking) and emotional clarity (how clearly emotions are expressed).

- Purpose of the Project
In today’s fast-paced digital world, individuals often struggle with:
Overthinking and repetitive thought patterns
Difficulty identifying and expressing emotions
Lack of self-awareness regarding mental and emotional states
MindMate AI aims to address these challenges by providing real-time insights into a user’s thought process using natural language analysis.
